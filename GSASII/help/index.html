Help for GSAS-II

This and the subsequent web pages provide documentation for the GSAS-II graphical user interface (GUI). The information is organized largely to follow the different sections of the GUI and is usually accessed via the "Help" features in GSAS-II. Help on specific sections of GSAS-II can be accessed from the yellow "?" button in the upper right corner of the data window. Access to specific help information is also offered on the graphics window and in other locations of the program.
